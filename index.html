Photo Editing

To design your own picture quote, upload your photo into BeFunky's Photo Editor and head to the Text tab. Click on the Add Text button or choose from the Text Presets options, which feature already stylized fonts that are ready to use with the click of a button. Start typing your quote into the text box that appears on your photo. Choose from a library of our fonts, or click on Yours to gain access to all of the fonts you have stored on your computer.
